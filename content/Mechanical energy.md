# Mechanical Energy
- Mech energy of flowing fluid per unit mass 
	- $e_{mech} = \frac P \rho + \frac {V^2} 2 + gz$
- Rate of mech energy of flowing fluid
	- $\dot{E_{mech}} = \dot{m}e_{mech} = \dot{m}(\frac P \rho + \frac {V^2} 2 + gz)$
- Mech energy change of a fluid during incrompressible flow per unit mass [kg/kg]
	- $\Delta e_{mech} = \frac{P_2-P_1}{\rho} + \frac{V_2^2-V_1^2}{2} + g(z_2 - z_1)$
- Rate of Mech energy change of a fluid during incrompressible flow per unit mass [kW]
	- $$	\begin{align} 
	\dot{E}_{mech} & = \dot{m}e_{mech} \\  
	 & = \dot{m}\Delta e_{mech} = \frac{P_2-P_1}{\rho} + \frac{V_2^2-V_1^2}{2} + g(z_2 - z_1) \\
	\end{align}
	$$

## Mechanical work
- $W = \int_{s_1}^{s_2}{F}{ds}$ [kJ]
	-  $= Fs$ when F constant
