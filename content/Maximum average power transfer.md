# Maximum average power transfer
## [[Direct current]] circuit
$P_{L}$  max when $R_L = R_{th}$

## [[Alternating current]] circuit
### Impedance load
$P_{Z_L}$ max when $Z_L = Z_{th}^*$

### Pure resistive load
$P_{R_L}$ max when $R_L = |Z_{th}|$.
